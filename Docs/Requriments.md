# FinGuard AI

## Problem Statement

Banks process millions of transactions every day. Detecting fraudulent transactions manually is slow, expensive, and error-prone. Existing fraud detection systems often provide only a risk score without explaining why a transaction is suspicious.

---

## Vision

Build an AI-powered Fraud Detection and Investigation Platform that predicts fraudulent transactions using Machine Learning and assists fraud investigators using Large Language Models (LLMs) with explainable AI reports and historical case retrieval.

---

## Target Users

- Fraud Analysts
- Fraud Investigators
- Risk Managers
- Bank Administrators

---

## Objectives

- Detect suspicious transactions
- Generate fraud risk score
- Explain why a transaction is suspicious
- Retrieve similar historical fraud cases
- Help investigators make decisions
- Maintain complete audit logs
